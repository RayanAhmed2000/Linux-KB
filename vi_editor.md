## ESC mode

- yy          (copy)
- p           (paste)
- SHIFT + A   (EOL)
- SHIFT + G   (EOF)
- gg          (start of file)
- dd          (delete current line)
- x           (delete one charecter)
- r           (digit replace)
- :x | :wq    (save and exit)

## Line substitution
- :%s/line1/line2/g      
(globally replace line1 with line2)
